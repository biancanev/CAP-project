What Are Eating Disorders?

In today’s modern society, appearances are becoming more of an essential factor of people's first impression. Now you may argue that people know that judging based on first looks is a bad thing, but the truth is everyone still does. Because of this, society gives a standards that if you have ANY visible fat on your body, you’re ugly and if you don’t, you’re pretty. People, especially teens, fall victim to such standards, trying to change their entire body structure just for the need to become pretty. But not only is the need to become skinny driven by the need to be pretty, it is also driven by the fear of bullying. The name we called the need to be skinny by starving yourself is anorexia, one of the many eating habits that are called eating disorders. When people want to look better and want to join the “popular group” they will try their hardest to look good. This can lead to health issues if they think they look to big. They will purge, force vomit, and even diet at a young age just to get thinner. This can lead to many issues know as “eating disorders”.
