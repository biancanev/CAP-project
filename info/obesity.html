What is Obesity

Obesity is being extremely overweight to the point of danger. Obesity can be extremely fatal, as the 5th leading disease in the whole world Other diseases related to obesity are diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure, some types of cancer, gallbladder diseases, osteoarthritis, gout, and breathing problems.

Can you "cure" obesity?

There is currently no way of curing obesity, however, you can prevent it by eating healthy and exercising regularly. This does NOT mean that you should starve yourself in order to prevent obesity. This will just lead to other problems that may even be worse than obesity.
